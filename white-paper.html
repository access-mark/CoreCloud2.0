Strategic Cloud Transformation in South Africa

Executive Summary

Cloud is not the future — it’s now. But for South African enterprises, cloud transformation isn't just about technology — it’s about scale, skills, and execution. This white paper outlines a bold, partner-enabled framework to migrate, modernize, manage, and monetize cloud at national scale.

Our 6-Phase Transformation Model

  • 1. Audit & Assess: Deep environment scans and stakeholder mapping
  • 2. Business Case & Roadmap: Defined TCO, ROI, and modernization milestones
  • 3. Cloud Migration: AWS-native migration with zero business disruption
  • 4. Modernization: Containerization, microservices, and DevSecOps enablement
  • 5. Optimization: FinOps, autoscaling, security, and performance tuning
  • 6. Managed Services: 24/7 SLAs, DevOps pipelines, and real-time insights

Strategic Pillars

CoreCloud’s strategy is rooted in four non-negotiables: Architecture, Talent, Partnerships, and Governance. These pillars ensure every engagement drives measurable outcomes, not promises.
