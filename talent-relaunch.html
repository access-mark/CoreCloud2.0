Why We Built This

South Africa’s job market is broken — and talent is being wasted. Young professionals are denied opportunity because they lack experience. Highly skilled professionals are excluded for being “too expensive” or “overqualified.” CoreCloud is changing that.

Re / Launch is more than a support page. It’s a platform to reclaim your career. We don’t do pity placements. We do purpose, partnership, and performance. This is how we rebuild careers, restore dignity, and reignite potential.
