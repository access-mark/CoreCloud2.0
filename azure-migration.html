Azure Migration Services

CoreCloud simplifies your journey to Microsoft Azure – helping you assess, plan, and migrate with unmatched clarity and precision.

Why Azure Migration?

As more organizations embrace hybrid and multi-cloud strategies, Microsoft Azure stands out as a secure, scalable, and intelligent cloud platform. But moving to Azure isn’t just about lift-and-shift — it’s about smart planning and execution. That’s where we come in.

Our Azure Migration Approach

  • Readiness Assessment: We evaluate your workloads, dependencies, licensing, and costs to determine migration feasibility.
  • Strategic Planning: We define your Azure landing zone and develop a migration roadmap aligned with your business goals.
  • Secure Migration Execution: We migrate applications and infrastructure using automation, encryption, and rollback safety measures.
  • Post-Migration Validation: We verify performance, compliance, and cost optimization — ensuring your Azure environment is ready to scale.

Use Cases

  • Data center exits and cloud-first initiatives
  • Moving from on-prem to Azure hybrid cloud
  • Disaster recovery and business continuity expansion
  • Migrate from AWS or GCP to Azure
